# SIGNAL TO NOISE - Quickplay Cheatsheet

## Setup (30 seconds)
- [ ] Deal 5 conspiracies face-up to center
- [ ] Each player gets 7 evidence cards (secret)
- [ ] Each player starts: **5 Credibility, 5 Audience**
- [ ] Choose first player randomly

---

## Win Condition
**After 6 rounds:** Highest Audience wins (tiebreak: Credibility)

**Instant Win:** Last player standing if others go bankrupt

---

## Round Structure

### Round 1 Only: TWO Investigate Phases!
All other rounds: ONE Investigate Phase

### 6 Phases Per Round

#### 1️⃣ INVESTIGATE
- Draw 3 cards (hand limit: 10)
- Assign cards from hand to conspiracies (secret)
- Click "Done" when ready

#### 2️⃣ ADVERTISE
- Signal ONE conspiracy publicly (or pass)
- **⚠️ Mismatch penalty:** -1 Audience if you broadcast differently later

#### 3️⃣ LATE-BREAKING EVIDENCE
- Play ONE card face-up (visible to all), OR
- Pass (keep hand secret)

#### 4️⃣ BROADCAST
- Choose ONE conspiracy to broadcast on (or pass)
- Declare position: REAL, FAKE, or INCONCLUSIVE
- Your evidence stays hidden until Resolve

#### 5️⃣ RESOLVE
**If consensus reached:**
1. Reveal all evidence on that conspiracy
2. Count proof values: REAL vs FAKE (ignore BLUFF cards)
3. Determine truth by majority proof
4. Score points or penalties

**If no consensus:** Skip to Cleanup

#### 6️⃣ CLEANUP
- Clear all queues and signals
- Advance round counter
- Rotate first player

---

## Consensus Thresholds

| Players | Needed |
|---------|--------|
| 3 | 2 |
| 4 | 2 |
| 5 | 3 |

**Requirements:**
- Same conspiracy
- Same position (REAL or FAKE)
- Not passed, not INCONCLUSIVE

---

## Scoring & Penalties

### ✅ If You Matched Truth
- **+3 Audience** (if you had ≥1 valid evidence)
- **-2 or -3 Credibility** (if you bluffed with 0 evidence)

### ⚠️ Bluffing Penalties (Cumulative!)
| Total Bluffs | Penalty |
|--------------|---------|
| 1st or 2nd | -2 Credibility |
| 3rd+ | -3 Credibility |

### 💀 Bankruptcy
**Credibility = 0 → ELIMINATED**

---

## Evidence Proof Values

| Type | Effect |
|------|--------|
| REAL | Counts for conspiracy being REAL |
| FAKE | Counts for conspiracy being FAKE |
| BLUFF | Worthless (doesn't count) |

---

## Quick Strategy Tips

### ✅ DO
- Keep credibility above 0 (avoid bankruptcy!)
- Match evidence to supported conspiracies
- Watch advertising signals for cooperation
- Track your total bluffs (penalties escalate!)
- Broadcast when you have evidence

### ❌ DON'T
- Don't bluff too often (penalties escalate!)
- Don't ignore hand limit (10 cards max)
- Don't mismatch advertise/broadcast (-1 Audience)
- Don't let Credibility hit 0 (elimination!)

## Common Mistakes

❌ Forgetting Round 1 has TWO Investigate phases
❌ Broadcasting without checking consensus threshold
❌ Ignoring bluff count (penalties escalate!)
❌ Playing evidence that doesn't support the conspiracy
❌ Going bankrupt (letting Credibility hit 0)

## Emergency Rules Lookup

**"Can I broadcast without evidence?"**
→ Yes! But if you match truth with 0 evidence = bluff penalty

**"What if we tie on revealed evidence?"**
→ No one scores, bluff penalties don't apply

**"Do INCONCLUSIVE broadcasts count?"**
→ No, only REAL/FAKE count toward consensus

**"Can I change assigned evidence?"**
→ No, assignments are final in this version

**"What if everyone passes broadcast?"**
→ No consensus, no scoring, proceed to cleanup

---

**Pro Tip:** Your first 2 bluffs are "cheap" (-2 Cred). Use them wisely early game when you have more credibility to spare!

**Remember:** Audience wins the game, but Credibility keeps you alive!

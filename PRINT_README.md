## 🖨️ How to Print

### For Best Results:

1. **Open HTML file** in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
2. **Print Settings** (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P):
   - **Paper Size**: A4
   - **Margins**: Minimal (or 10mm)
   - **Scale**: 100% (do not scale)
   - **Color**: Color recommended for badges, or Grayscale works great too!
   - **Background Graphics**: Optional (cards have white backgrounds)

3. **Two-Sided Printing**:
   - **Conspiracy Cards**: Print pages 1-2 (fronts), flip paper, print pages 3-4 (backs)
   - **Evidence Cards**: Print odd pages (fronts), flip paper, print even pages (backs)
   - Align carefully for professional double-sided cards!

4. **Paper Recommendations**:
   - **Best**: 200-250gsm cardstock
   - **Good**: 160gsm cardstock
   - **Budget**: 120gsm cardstock + card sleeves
   - **Standard**: 80gsm paper + card sleeves (works fine!)

## ✂️ Cutting Guide

Each page contains **9 cards** in a **3×3 grid**:
- Card size: ~64mm × ~94mm (close to poker card size)
- Gap between cards: 3mm
- Total cutting per page: 12 cuts (6 horizontal, 6 vertical)

**Tips**:
- Use a paper cutter for straight edges
- Cut guides available: subtle borders on each card
- Consider card sleeves (63.5mm × 88mm) for protection

## 🎨 Color vs Black & White

### Color Printing (Recommended)
- **Tier badges** show proper colors (green/orange/red)
- **Excitement badges** are color-coded (red/gray/light gray)
- **Proof values** have colored backgrounds (green/red/yellow)
- More visually appealing and easier to distinguish at a glance

### Black & White / Grayscale (Works Great!)
- **Fully readable** - all text is black on white
- Tier indicators use star symbols (★/★★/★★★)
- Excitement symbols are clear (★★★ / ★☆☆ / ☆☆☆)
- Proof values still distinguishable with ✓/✗/🎭 symbols
- **Very ink-efficient** - white backgrounds save tons of ink!

📡 SIGNAL TO NOISE — Quick Reference

Game Flow (6 rounds)

1. COMMIT — Secretly assign evidence to conspiracies (others see count, not cards)
2. BROADCAST — In turn order, declare REAL/FAKE on one conspiracy, or PASS
3. RESOLVE — Check consensus, score, replace resolved conspiracies, draw 2 cards

Scoring

SituationPoints
Majority + evidence3
...specific evidence bonus+1
Majority, no evidence (bandwagon)2
First broadcaster bonus+1
Per extra player on majority beyond threshold+1 each
Minority / No consensus / Pass0

Consensus Threshold

3-4 players: 2 needed | 5 players: 3 needed

Credibility

+1 majority, -1 minority. Range 0-10. Start at 5. Tiebreaker only.

Key Numbers

  • 5 active conspiracies on the board
  • Starting hand: 5 cards | Draw: 2/round | Max hand: 7
  • Cards: 12 generic (any conspiracy) + 36 specific (named conspiracy)
  • Lowest score goes first each round

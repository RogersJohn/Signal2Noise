# Signal to Noise - Quick Reference

**Players:** 3-5 | **Rounds:** 6 | **Win:** Highest audience score after 6 rounds

---

## 🎯 5 Phases Per Round

| Phase | Color | What You Do |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **🔍 INVESTIGATE** | Blue | Secretly assign evidence cards to conspiracies |
| **📢 ADVERTISE** | Purple | Signal which conspiracy interests you (or pass) |
| **📻 BROADCAST** | Orange | Make public claim: REAL / FAKE / INCONCLUSIVE |
| **⚖️ RESOLVE** | Green | Consensus checked, points scored, credibility adjusted |
| **🧹 CLEANUP** | Gray | Revealed conspiracies replaced, draw cards |

---

## 📊 Scoring Formula

```
Base Points:
  3 = With evidence
  1 = Bandwagoning (no evidence)
  2 = INCONCLUSIVE (always safe)

+ Evidence Bonuses (per card):
  Specificity: +3 (specific) or +1 (ALL cards)
  × Excitement: ×2.0 (★★★), ×1.0 (★☆☆), ×0.5 (☆☆☆, rounds UP)
  Novelty: +2 (first use)

× Credibility Modifier:
  ×1.5 (credibility 7-10)
  ×1.0 (credibility 4-6)
  ×0.75 (credibility 0-3)

= Total Audience Points
```

## 🎭 Credibility Changes

| Situation | Credibility Change |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Bluffing** (no real evidence) | **Escalating:** -2, -3, -4, -5 (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th+ bluff) |
| Majority side (consensus reached) | +1 |
| Minority side (consensus reached) | -3 |
| INCONCLUSIVE broadcast | No change |
| Advertise deception | -1 **audience** (not credibility) |

**Key:** First bluff costs -2 credibility. Serial bluffing escalates quickly!

## 🤝 Consensus Thresholds

| Players | Votes Needed |
|---------|--------------|
| 3 players | 2 |
| 4 players | 2 |
| 5 players | 3 |

**INCONCLUSIVE broadcasts don't count toward consensus!**

## 📢 ADVERTISE Phase Rules

1. **Public signal** - Everyone sees your advertisement
2. **Not binding** - You can broadcast elsewhere
3. **Deception penalty** - Advertise conspiracy A, broadcast B = **-1 audience**
4. **Bonus evidence** - After all ads, **place 1 card** anywhere
5. **Pass option** - Keep intentions secret (no penalty)

---

## 🎴 Evidence Cards

**Specificity:**
- **Specific cards** support 1-5 conspiracies → +3 bonus
- **ALL cards** support any conspiracy → +1 bonus

**Excitement Multiplier:**
- ★★★ **EXCITING** (×2.0): Doubles your specificity bonus!
- ★☆☆ **NEUTRAL** (×1.0): No modifier
- ☆☆☆ **BORING** (×0.5): Half value, rounds **UP** (3→2, 1→1)

**Novelty:**
- First time using a card on any conspiracy: +2 bonus
- Already used: No novelty bonus

## 🏆 Strategy Tips

### Beginners
✓ Broadcast where you have evidence
✓ Watch advertisements for consensus
✓ Join the majority
✓ Use INCONCLUSIVE when unsure
✓ Protect credibility (avoid lone broadcasts)

### Advanced
✓ Bluff occasionally (-2 is manageable)
✓ Advertise honestly to coordinate
✓ Advertise deceptively to set traps (-1 audience)
✓ Ride majority bonuses to build credibility
✓ Track who follows their advertisements

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ **Serial bluffing** - Escalates to -4 or -5 per bluff
❌ **Ignoring credibility** - Dropping to 0-3 = -25% scoring penalty
❌ **Broadcasting alone** - Risk -3 credibility if no consensus
❌ **Forgetting advertisements** - Deception costs -1 audience
❌ **Wasting excitement** - Exciting cards are worth 2× the bonus!

## 🎲 Quick Decision Guide

**Should I broadcast?**
- Have evidence? → YES (3 base points)
- Ads suggest consensus? → MAYBE (bandwagon 1 point)
- Uncertain? → INCONCLUSIVE (safe 2 points)
- No evidence + no ads? → PASS

**Should I bluff?**
- First bluff? → Risky but viable (-2 credibility)
- Second bluff? → Very risky (-3 credibility)
- Third+ bluff? → DON'T (-4/-5 credibility)

**Should I advertise honestly?**
- Want to coordinate? → YES (safe consensus)
- Want to trap opponents? → NO (-1 audience to you)
- Unsure of strategy? → PASS (no penalty)

---

## 📏 Turn Sequence Reminder

1. **INVESTIGATE**: Assign cards → Everyone draws 2
2. **ADVERTISE**: Signal conspiracy → Place 1 bonus card
3. **BROADCAST**: Make claim (REAL/FAKE/INCONCLUSIVE/PASS)
4. **RESOLVE**: Check consensus → Score → Adjust credibility
5. **CLEANUP**: Replace revealed → Check win condition → Next round

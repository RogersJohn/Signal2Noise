# Signal to Noise - Printing Instructions

## Overview
This document provides instructions for printing the physical game components for **Signal to Noise**.

## Files Included
1. `printable-cards-conspiracy-REVISED.html` - Conspiracy Cards + Truth Indicators (5 pages)
   - 12 conspiracy card fronts (no truth values visible)
   - 12 REAL truth indicators (green, for variant mode)
   - 12 FAKE truth indicators (red, for variant mode)
2. `printable-cards-evidence.html` - Evidence Cards (78 cards, 9 pages)
3. `printable-player-tokens.html` - Broadcast tokens and evidence markers (5 player sets, 2 pages)
4. `printable-player-boards.html` - Player reference boards for tracking credibility and audience (5 boards, 2 pages)

## Card Specifications
- **Card Size**: 2.5" × 3.5" (standard poker playing card size)
- **Cards per page**: 9 (3×3 grid)
- **Page size**: US Letter (8.5" × 11")

## What You'll Need
- Printer (color recommended for best results)
- Cardstock paper (65-110 lb / 176-300 gsm recommended)
- Paper cutter or scissors
- Optional: Card sleeves (2.5" × 3.5" / 63mm × 88mm)
- Optional: Laminator for durability

## Printing Instructions

### Step 1: Open the Files
1. Open `printable-cards-conspiracy-REVISED.html` in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.)
2. The file should display the cards in a print-ready layout

### Step 2: Print Settings
When printing, use these settings:

**For Conspiracy Cards + Truth Indicators (5 pages):**
- Pages to print: All (1-5)
- Page 1-2: Conspiracy card fronts (12 cards, NO truth values visible)
- Page 3: REAL truth indicator cards (12 green cards for "Truth Matters" variant mode)
- Page 4: FAKE truth indicator cards (12 red cards for "Truth Matters" variant mode)
- Page 5: Card backs (optional)
- **NOTE**: Truth indicators are inserted into card sleeves BEHIND conspiracy cards for variant mode only

**For Evidence Cards (9 pages):**
- Pages to print: All (1-9)
- All pages contain card fronts (last page includes 3 card backs)

**For Player Tokens (2 pages):**
- Pages to print: All (1-2)
- Contains broadcast tokens (REAL, FAKE, ???) and evidence markers for 5 players

**For Player Boards (2 pages):**
- Pages to print: All (1-2)
- Landscape orientation
- Contains 5 player reference boards for tracking credibility (0-10) and audience points

**Print Settings:**
- Color: Color (recommended) or Grayscale
- Paper Size: Letter (8.5" × 11")
- Margins: 0.25" (already built into the layout)
- Scale: 100% (do NOT use "Fit to page")
- Background graphics: ON (to preserve card colors and styling)

### Step 3: Paper Selection
For best results:
- **Budget option**: 65 lb cardstock (lightweight but functional)
- **Recommended**: 80-110 lb cardstock (good durability)
- **Premium**: Print on regular paper + insert into card sleeves with backing cards

### Step 4: Cutting
1. Use a paper cutter for straight, clean cuts
2. Each card is 2.5" × 3.5"
3. Cut along the visible borders between cards
4. Alternative: Use scissors for manual cutting

### Step 5: Optional Enhancements

#### Card Sleeves
- Size: 2.5" × 3.5" (63mm × 88mm) - Standard poker size
- Benefit: Protects cards, adds durability, makes shuffling easier
- Can insert cheaper paper into sleeves with a backing card

#### Lamination
- Use a pouch laminator with 2.5" × 3.5" pouches
- Provides water resistance and durability
- May make cards slightly larger

#### Corner Rounding
- Use a corner rounder punch
- Makes cards look more professional
- Prevents corners from bending

## Printing Tips

### For Double-Sided Printing
1. Print all front faces first
2. Flip the paper stack (check your printer's orientation)
3. Print the back pages
4. Test with 1 page first to ensure proper alignment

### Color vs Black & White
- **Color**: Recommended for the best experience
  - Conspiracy cards have color-coded tiers (Tier 1: tan, Tier 2: red, Tier 3: blue)
  - Evidence cards have excitement levels (boring: gray, neutral: yellow, exciting: orange)
  - Truth indicators color-coded (REAL: green, FAKE: red) for variant mode
  - Player tokens color-coded (Blue, Red, Green, Purple, Orange)

- **Black & White**: Still functional
  - All information is readable in grayscale
  - Tier and excitement levels indicated by text labels
  - Player colors labeled on tokens

### Test Print
Before printing all pages:
1. Print page 1 of conspiracy cards on regular paper
2. Verify that cards are the correct size (2.5" × 3.5")
3. Check that all text is readable
4. Adjust printer settings if needed

## Component Breakdown

### Conspiracy Cards (12 total)
**BASE GAME**: Conspiracy cards have NO truth values visible on them
- **Tier 1**: 6 cards (yellow/tan background)
- **Tier 2**: 4 cards (red/pink background)
- **Tier 3**: 2 cards (blue/purple background)

**"TRUTH MATTERS" VARIANT**: Truth indicator cards are separate
- 12 REAL indicators (green cards) - Insert behind conspiracy cards in sleeves
- 12 FAKE indicators (red cards) - Insert behind conspiracy cards in sleeves
- In base game, consensus determines outcome, NOT objective truth
- Truth indicators only used for optional "Truth Matters" variant mode

### Evidence Cards (78 total)
- **Excitement Levels**:
  - BORING (☆☆☆): Generic, works with all conspiracies, but less engaging
  - NEUTRAL (★☆☆): Moderate excitement
  - EXCITING (★★★): High excitement, often specific to certain conspiracies

- **Card Distribution**:
  - Generic evidence (supports ALL conspiracies): ~40 cards
  - Specific evidence (supports specific conspiracies): ~38 cards

### Player Tokens (5 player sets)
Each player color receives:
- 1 REAL broadcast token (green checkmark)
- 1 FAKE broadcast token (red X)
- 1 INCONCLUSIVE broadcast token (??? symbol)
- 15 evidence markers (circular tokens with player color)

**Player Colors**: Blue, Red, Green, Purple, Orange

### Player Boards (5 boards)
Each board tracks:
- **Credibility Track**: 0-10 scale (starts at 5)
- **Audience Track**: Victory points (starts at 0)
- **Round Tracker**: Rounds 1-6
- **Reference**: Quick rules summary

## Assembly Recommendations

### Basic Assembly
1. Print all pages on cardstock
2. Cut out all cards
3. Organize into two decks (Conspiracy and Evidence)
4. Ready to play!

### Premium Assembly
1. Print on regular paper
2. Cut out all cards
3. Insert into card sleeves with backing cards from old playing cards
4. Organize into decks
5. Store in deck boxes or card storage

## Storage Suggestions
- Use deck boxes (for sleeved cards)
- Use rubber bands or card boxes (for unsleeved cards)
- Keep conspiracy cards separate from evidence cards
- Consider labeling containers

## Troubleshooting

### Cards are too small/large
- Check that print scale is set to 100%
- Verify paper size is set to Letter (8.5" × 11")
- Check that margins are NOT overriding the layout

### Colors don't match screen
- Enable "Background graphics" in print settings
- Some color variation is normal between screen and printer
- Adjust printer color settings if needed

### Text is cut off
- Verify margins are set to 0.25"
- Check that "Fit to page" is OFF
- Try printing from a different browser
